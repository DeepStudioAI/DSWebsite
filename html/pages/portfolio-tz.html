Simplifying how farmers search for farm equipment auctions


Farm equipment auctions in the palm of your hand

Ionic 2  /  Laravel  /  Rest APIs  /  Angular JS 4

As his passion for agriculture grew Tractor Zoom’s founder, Kyle, recognized farmers didn’t have a digitally searchable database for upcoming farm equipment auctions. The automotive and real estate industries had them. ‘For Sale’ farm equipment had them… but why not farm equipment auctions? Kyle turned to VenturePoint to help redefine the way people find farm equipment auctions.
