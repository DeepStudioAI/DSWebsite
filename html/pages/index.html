Meet the Team


Kenny Lee

CEO

Kenny is responsible for VenturePoint’s vision, values and corporate innovation strategy. He previously founded Airtus Inc., a technology venture focused on optimizing commercial cargo space for air carriers using predictive technologies, which was acquired in 2016. He served as COO and SVP of Innovation for Globatom, a supply chain technology company focused on predictive logistics (AI for Supply Chain) and tracking of transportation assets, which was venture backed. Prior to that, Kenny was with Amazon and managed 8 countries for Amazon’s Worldwide Logistics and Transportation team. Kenny holds a B.A. in finance and economics from Western Washington University.

Thomas Hornbeck

CTO

Thomas is advancing technology and innovating products at VenturePoint by leading research at the intersection of statistics, machine learning, and big data. Prior to VenturePoint, Thomas was the Co-Founder of GotIt! (world's leading on-demand AI-powered platform for knowledge), which raised over $10M from prominent Silicon Valley VCs and produced mobile apps that achieved top rankings in over 150 countries. Thomas holds computer science degrees from the University of Iowa (B.S, M.S., and PhD abd). Thomas also teaches an entrepreneurship-focused mobile development course at the University of Iowa's Department of Computer Science.

Atakan Seckin

Head of Product

Atakan is an award-winning UI/UX designer who leads product strategy, execution and innovation at VenturePoint. Previously, he worked at Google on Google Shopping and on the UX Research team. Prior to Google he successfully brought to market several early stage high growth B2C products such as GotIt!'s on-demand learning app. After working in Mountain View at Google, he led UX on many projects at a major healthcare technology company, Pulse Systems, Inc. Atakan has been a major advisor and contributor to many early-stage startups in the U.S. and internationally. Atakan has a bachelors in Visual Communication Design from the University of Bilgi.
